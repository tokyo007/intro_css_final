This is a list of popular mid-cap stocks listed in the United States

According to the Investopedia Website, mid-cap stocks are stocks with market capitalization between 2 billion USD and 10 billion USD.

Recently, many large-cap stocks are very expensive based on various valuation meaures. In addition, the overall stock market performance has come from just a handful of large-cap stocks. For investors, worried about future returns of these large-cap stocks, it might be beneficial to look at stocks in the mid-cap range. Many of these stocks are well-known companies and offer solid growth.
